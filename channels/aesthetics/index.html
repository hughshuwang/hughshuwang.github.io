Preface

Inspired by Frank Ocean, Justin Vernon, and Kanye West, I’m deeply attracted by untraditional arts yielding emotional impacts and meditative experience. Soul and ambient soundtracks can grow with memories natually and serve as a way to spiritual flashbacks. The desire to appreciate and learn the spectacular creativity in our generation is the most supportive power in my life.

The “Aesthetics” channel was the very first reason why I started project “cyaned”, as sharing mental experience can amplify the happiness itself greatly. I play electric guitar, keyboard, synthesizer and collect artworks in my spare time. I have a vinyl collection with over 60 albums and got Lucifer, 1947 by Jackson Pollock and The Ballerina by George Condo hung on the walls of my bedroom.
