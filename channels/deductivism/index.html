Bio

Shu received his Master’s degree in finance/financial mathematics from Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and his Bachelor’s degree in finance from Jiao Tong University. He also spent part of his college life at University of California, Berkeley and Stanford University as an exchange student and a certificated research trainee. Prior to college, he was a national finalist in Chinese Physics Olympiad (CPhO) and made it in China top 150.

After graduation, he has been working as a Research Associate with Prof. Leonid Kogan at 55 Institutional Partners, LLC, Boston, for a year, focusing on topics in portfolio optimization, applications of mathematical programming, as well as quantitative strategy development.
