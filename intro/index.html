Preface

Welcome. I’m Shu. Glad you came.

I believe in Occam’s Razor, so I tried to build this site in the simplest way possible, and only show what’s necessary for you to know about myself, all the cool things I’m into, and, possibly, to discover any resemblance between us, so that we could be friends.

You might ask, why this name “CYANED” instead of “The Portfolio of Shu Shean”? It’s simple, cyan, a greenish blue, is my favourite color. It’s the color of sky, water, ocean, and Neptune. Why the ‘ed’ suffix? Cuz I love Frank Ocean, whose official is under blonded.co with the name “BLONDED” - it’s everything to me.

Life is short, I spend my time wisely, and look forward to experiencing different forms of “being a human”: as a mathematician, an engineer, a programmer, a dancer, a singer, a photographer, a novelist, a guitar player, a producer, a traveler, a snowboarder, and so many others. I’m actively exploring my interests and refuse to settle with anything, even those I’ve been always good at.

Now, maybe it’s better to split things up a bit so you can walk around on this spacy canvas and see if anything catches you.
